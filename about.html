Hi! My name is Crystal Harpstreit. I'm a graphic and web designer. I focus on creating beautiful, sleek and intuitive logos and websites for individual clients and small businesses. I began my journey into web design in 2013 in San Francisco, CA where I was able to learn so much from talented designers from all over the world. I've created pieces for everything from large corporations like Specialized Bicycle Components to non-profit organizations like Felidae for their Bay Area Puma Project . I understand good, clean design and what it takes get noticed. Let me help you get there.
